The Grays Inn Story

A private address for a well-kept stay

Set behind gated grounds off Ogombo Road in Lekki Phase 2, Grays Inn Hotel was built for travellers who want comfort without noise. Every room is considered, every corner is looked after, and every guest is greeted like they've stayed before.

From the moment you arrive at our gate to the moment you check out, our team handles the details — so your time in Lagos feels unhurried, secure, and entirely your own.
