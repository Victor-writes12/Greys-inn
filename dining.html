The Restaurant

Continental & local, done simply

Our kitchen keeps things honest — a proper breakfast to start the day, and a continental and local menu for lunch and dinner. Whether you're in for a quiet solo meal or hosting a small business dinner, the dining room is set up to feel unhurried.

  • Breakfast Daily, 7:00 – 10:30am
  • Lunch & Dinner Daily, 12:00 – 10:00pm
  • Room Service 24 Hours

The Bar

Cocktails, quietly poured

Pull up a stool at the brick-lined bar or take a low table out on the covered patio. Our bar keeps a tight, well-made list — classic cocktails, cold beer, and a short wine selection — served late into the evening.

  • Cocktail Bar 4:00pm – Late
  • Wine Collection Curated, by the glass or bottle
  • Outdoor Patio Seating Available Nightly
