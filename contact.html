Before You Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in?

Check-in is from 2:00pm and check-out by 12:00pm. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged on request, subject to availability.

Is parking available?

Yes — secure, gated parking is available on-site for all guests, free of charge.

Do you offer airport pickup?

Airport pickup can be arranged in advance — mention it in your booking request and our team will confirm details.

Can I book the restaurant without staying?

Yes, the restaurant and bar are open to walk-in guests as well as hotel residents.
